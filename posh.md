**1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?**

Sexual harassment is any type of sexual behavior that makes a person feel uncomfortable, unsafe. It can include asking for sexual favors for promotions, telling sexual jokes, sending bad messages or pictures, or touching someone without their permission. Even if someone says it was a joke, it is harassment if it makes the other person uncomfortable.

---

**2. What would you do if you face or witness such behaviour?**

If I face it, I would clearly tell the person to stop. And  I would write down what happened and report it to my manager or HR.

If I see it happening to someone else, I would support them and encourage them to report it.
